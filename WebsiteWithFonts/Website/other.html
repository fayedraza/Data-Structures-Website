Towers of Hanoi

Suppose we have a situation where we want to move a stack of discs from one pole to another. The discs are stacked from the biggest disc to the smallest disc. You can look on the image below and see that we want to move from pole A to pole C. And pole B will be like our helper pole. Here are two rules: 1. We can more one disk at a time and 2. a bigger disk can not go on top of a smaller stack.

General Trees

Back to top

General Tree is a type of tree where it has as many children as possible. It can be used to model hierarchies. Like the top might be a king or queen. Bottom might be a prince or princess and so on.
